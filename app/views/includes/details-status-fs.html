Show definitions of statuses

Standards that are proposed are suggestions for new standards where there is a clear gap but no further work has been done, such as identifying a lead developer.

Standards that are draft in progress are in the process of being developed or consulted on by the lead developer.

Standards that are awaiting approval have finalised documentation and are being quality checked by the Data Alliance Partnership Board.

Standards that are on hold have been paused but are expected to resume in the future.

Standards that have been cancelled are no longer being considered for publication as an Information Standard Notice.
